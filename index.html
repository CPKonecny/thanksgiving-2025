House Guide - Everything You Need to Know

‚òï Kitchen & Food

  • Coffee Maker: The coffee maker is set to brew every morning at 5:30 AM - the first coffee will be ready to drink around 5:45 AM. There will also be a large carafe of hot coffee available throughout the day. Please help yourself to as much coffee as you want! Cold brew is also available in the refrigerator. Creamer can be found in the refrigerator and sugar and honey can be found at the coffee station. Please use disposable paper cups provided.
  • Cups: If you need a cup - grab one of the solo cups found at the coffee station and write your name on it.
  • Microwave: Located on top of the garage refrigerator
  • Fridge: The garage refrigerator is stocked with different drinks for you to enjoy - help yourself!
  • Dishwasher Etiquette: We will handle unloading the dishwasher - if you want to load the dishwasher go for it! We use Blueland tablets that can be found under the kitchen sink. Our dishwasher is very quiet - it's hard to tell if it's running. If you see the orange/red light - it's running!
  • Utensils: Find the trash can - one drawer to your left
  • Trash/Recycling: We don't recycle - our trash can is a pull out drawer and if your hands are dirty you can use your foot to open it (there's a little lever). Feel free to put trash in both cans. If you want to take the trash out - our BLUE roll out trash cans can be found next to the driveway. Extra trash bags are to the left of the oven in the bottom drawer.

üè† House Basics

  • WiFi Password: Konecny
  • Thermostat: There is nothing worse than being in someone's home and being too cold or too hot so if you are uncomfortable - let us know - seriously! We keep it COLD and we know that is not everyone's preference. You are our guests and we want you to be comfortable!
  • Door Locks: Our doors lock automatically - you'll hear them locking - it's kinda weird at first.
  • Bathrooms: We are on a septic system that means the only thing that can be flushed is toilet paper - and nature... so put everything else in the trash can please.
  • Washer/Dryer: If you need to use the washer and dryer come see JC - she's happy to help you!
  • Extra Towels/Linens: Your room/bed is provided with fresh linens. Every person will get their own towels :) Just be sure to hang your towel somewhere to dry so it's ready for you when you need it next.
  • Parking: Plenty of room in driveway and in front of house

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I pack?

  • Casual comfortable clothes
  • Layers (it can be chilly in the mornings/evenings)
  • Walking shoes if you want to hike
  • Any specialty toiletries you need
  • Medications
  • Phone chargers

What time should we arrive Wednesday?

Arrive whenever works for you! Just text when you're getting close so we can be ready to welcome you.

Should we bring anything?

Just yourselves! If you want to volunteer for a side dish or dessert, check the menu section above. Feel free to bring any favorite beverages or snacks you'd like.

What about allergies/dietary restrictions?

We'll have GF options. Please let us know about any other restrictions ASAP.

Can I help cook?

Absolutely! We'd love the help, especially with side dishes. Most of you enjoy cooking, so this will be fun.

Is there good cell service?

Yes! All major carriers work well. WiFi is also excellent.

What if I need to do some work?

Plenty of quiet spaces and good WiFi. The office, bedrooms, or even coffee shops nearby work great.
